文字是人类交流的方式，是我和我自己交流的方式。
是我的日记簿，我通过文字写下自己的所见所闻。
是检查自己生活的一个反思过程。
是放松的过程。
是我的记忆力。

Words is the way that people communicate, is they way i communicate with myself.
It's my notebook, I wrote down what i heard and seen.
It's a process of introspection, life inspection.
A process of relax.
My memory.
