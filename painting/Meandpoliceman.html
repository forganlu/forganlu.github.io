Me and policeman | Collage & sketch
Oct. 2018



I hate my father.

He always tells me to do this or to do that, I don't like to be told to do anything. But one day I realized, it was not he wants me to the thing he want, it just he want to do something, but he was so shy to do it, so he asked me to represent him to finish this jobs.

Once I knew the reason, I found I actually don't hate him that much. He is also the first time to be people's father. I love him, my dear policeman.
